## Tarea 4: Scanner G1 — llenar `bot.strat_f_panel` desde `_strat_f_batch`
**Objective:** Cada ciclo, el panel STRAT-F se actualiza en vivo.
**Files:** Modify `src/scanner.py` (bloque 1577-1612, dentro del `finally` o tras el loop).
**Step 1 (impl):** tras procesar el batch (antes del `finally self._strat_f_batch=None`),
mapear recs a `StratFRow`/`StratFReject` y llamar `record_strat_f`:
```python
from hub.strat_f_state import StratFRow, StratFReject
accepted_rows, rejected_rows = [], []
for _rec in (self._strat_f_batch or []):
    if _rec.get("decision") == "ACCEPTED":
        accepted_rows.append(StratFRow(
            asset=_rec["asset"], direction=_rec.get("direction", ""),
            strength=int(round((_rec.get("strength", 0.0) or 0.0) * 100)),
            payout=int(_rec.get("payout", 0) or 0),
            ctx=_rec.get("ctx", ""), event=_rec.get("event", "")))
    else:
        rejected_rows.append(StratFReject(
            asset=_rec["asset"], payout=int(_rec.get("payout", 0) or 0),
            skip_reason=_rec.get("skip_reason", "") or _rec.get("reject_reason", "") or ""))
try:
    self.bot.strat_f_panel.record_strat_f(
        accepted=accepted_rows, rejected=rejected_rows,
        total_assets=getattr(self, "_last_scan_asset_count", 0))
except Exception as _panel_err:
    log.warning("[HUB] Panel STRAT-F no actualizado: %s", _panel_err)
```
(Nota: `self._strat_f_batch` es lista de dicts con claves asset/direction/strength/
payout/ctx/event/decision/skip_reason — confirmado en scanner.py:1208-1263.)
